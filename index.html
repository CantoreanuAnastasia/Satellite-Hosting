How it works

Don't worry! We will guide you at every step and answer any questions you have

  • 1.Sign up: Creating an account on our website can provide several benefits like personalization, access to additional features, easy checkout, order tracking and better communication.

  • 2.Select a hosting plan: You need to select a hosting plan that meets your website's needs. Different plans offer different amounts of storage, bandwidth, and other features.

  • 3.Upload your website: After you've selected a hosting plan, you can upload your website to our servers. This is typically done through a file transfer protocol (FTP) client, which allows you to transfer files from your computer to the server.

  • 4.Configure your website: Once your website is uploaded, you'll need to configure it to work with our servers. This may involve setting up a domain name, configuring email accounts, and other tasks.

  • 5.Launch your website: Once everything is set up and configured, you're ready to launch your website! You can now share your website with the world and start attracting visitors.
